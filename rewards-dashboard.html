Roady's Rewards — Action Plan
Structured around: Fuel Gallon Performance · Member Value & Retention · Operational Efficiency
Fuel Gallons Member Retention Operational Efficiency
📊 1. Current State
Fuel Gallons
YTD loyalty gallons down 21.3% vs 2025 (12.47M vs 15.84M). Fewer gallons captured means less Rewards activity — the program engine is stalling. 7 locations show zero activity this month.
Member Value & Retention
Redemption rate collapsed to 60.25% in March (from 74.94% in Feb). 11 locations are below the 50% threshold. When drivers can't or don't redeem, they disengage and stop coming back.
Operational Efficiency
With 7 inactive locations and 11 below 50%, significant network capacity is unused. Locations missing signage, trained staff, working tablets, or cards are invisible to drivers. These are fixable operational failures.
⚠️
2. Biggest Problem — March Redemption Collapse + Declining Engagement
The 14.7pp drop in redemption rate from February to March (74.94% → 60.25%) is the single most alarming signal. Combined with adds down 25.8% YoY in March, the program is losing both sides of the equation simultaneously. The root cause: locations are not operationally ready to support the program — no signage, undertrained staff, broken/missing tablets, and no rewards card inventory.
💰 3. Opportunity Size
→If 2026 YTD Adds matched 2025 pace: +$43,562 in additional program volume (24% recovery)
→If March redemption rate recovered to 74.94% (Feb level): +$13,362 more in redeems, improving driver loyalty metrics
→7 inactive locations activated at average network rate: estimated +$7,000–$14,000/month in program adds
→11 below-50% locations brought to 70% redemption rate: significant improvement in driver retention and repeat visits
→21.3% gallon volume recovery at loyalty pricing: meaningful revenue and fleet loyalty impact across the network
4. Plan — 5 Actions
🪧
Action 01 · Operational
Signage Audit & Deployment
Every active location must have Roady's Rewards signage posted at the pump, at the register, and at the entrance. Drivers can't enroll in a program they don't know exists.
  • Audit all 50 network locations for signage presence and condition
  • Ship signage kits to any location missing materials within 2 weeks
  • Prioritize the 7 zero-activity and 11 below-50% locations first
  • Confirm placement at pump islands, POS counter, and entrance
🎓
Action 02 · Operational
Full Staff Training Sweep
Undertrained staff = missed enrollments and failed redemptions. Every location must have at least one fully trained staff member per shift who can enroll drivers and process redemptions.
  • Complete Roady's Training Academy Rewards module for all location staff
  • Focus on enrollment scripts, POS redemption flow, and dispute handling
  • Prioritize 18 locations below 70% redemption rate — likely a training gap
  • Verify training completion via Training Academy per-user tracking
📱
Action 03 · Operational
Tablet & POS Integration Check
A location without a working tablet or POS integration cannot process Rewards correctly. This is the fastest way to explain why some locations show zero or near-zero activity.
  • Audit every location — confirm tablet is present, charged, and operational
  • Identify which locations are POS-integrated vs tablet-only
  • Escalate broken/offline tablets for immediate replacement or repair
  • Priority: all 7 zero-activity locations — likely a hardware issue
  • Work toward POS integration at high-volume tablet-only locations
💳
Action 04 · Member Retention
Rewards Card Inventory Replenishment
If a location runs out of physical Rewards cards, they cannot enroll new members. Low card inventory is a silent program killer that directly reduces add transactions.
  • Survey all locations for current card inventory levels
  • Establish a minimum stock threshold (e.g. 50 cards per location)
  • Expedite card shipments to locations reporting low or zero inventory
  • Set up auto-replenishment trigger when stock drops below threshold
📈
Action 05 · Fuel Gallons · Member Retention
Growth Strategist Outreach — Low Performers
Submit data on the 11 below-50% and 7 zero-activity locations to the Growth Strategist for direct outreach. Personal contact with location managers drives accountability and program re-engagement faster than email blasts.
  • Export the 18 priority locations (11 below 50% + 7 zero activity) with Add $, Redeem $, Rate, and % change
  • Submit to Growth Strategist with context: gallons trend, missed opportunity estimate
  • Growth Strategist makes direct contact with each location manager
  • Goal: confirm operational readiness (signage, tablet, cards, training) on each call
  • Track outreach outcomes — re-check each location's rate the following month
